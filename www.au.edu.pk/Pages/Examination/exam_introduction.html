Examination Department Introduction Guidelines

Introduction

The Examination department at AU is an integral part of university administration. AU Examination department is responsible for all matters concerning the conduct of examinations, marking, compilation and declaration of results and maintaining the record thereof. Headed by the Director Examination, the examination department provides timely & secure services related to its domain. It believes in integrity, secrecy and quality services as its core values. The integrity of the examination process depends on everyone respecting the established rules and accepting their responsibilities in a consistent manner. Accordingly, various policies & procedures have been developed as per our requirements & available resources to establish a fair, appropriate and transparent examination system, free from corruption & malpractices, in line with the best practices.
