Programs Offered

Bachelor Programs

  • Bachelor of Science in Gaming Multimedia

    This program is designed to develop students’ strong base in computing and give them a set of gaming and animation skills to design, deploy, and manage multimedia systems. The courses are carefully incorporated from multiple disciplines, such as mathematics, and humanities, to prepare students from every aspect. The computing and gaming & multimedia specialized courses are designed as a blend of theory and lab work; to provide deep understanding of the technical concepts and hand-on tools.

    Programme Objectives

    Bachelors of Gaming and Multimedia programme is designed with specific aims in mind that are mentioned follows:

    • Provide students with fundamental computing, gaming, animation, virtual reality, multimedia, mathematics, and humanity courses which are essential to develop strong knowledge base of computing and gaming and multimedia experts and researchers.
    • Produce gaming, animation and virtual reality specialists to meet the current and future demands of the country and capture international multimedia market.
    • Attract International jobs/companies by creating a local pool of talented and skilled professionals.
    • Contribute toward establishing Pakistan gaming, animation and virtual reality industry.
    • Make Air University a pioneer in this field at Pakistan level and one of the renowned Universities for multimedia internationally.
    • Attract international students from the neighbouring countries to bring good name to Air University and help improve the University ranking
    • Produce graduates that are more employable and highly paid.
  • Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering

    The aim of Department of Computer Science is to conduct “Bachelor of Science (Software Engineering) Program with the objective to produce competent Software Engineers and researchers to face the technological challenges of 21st century”. A software engineering program develops professionals who have a mastery of software development principles, theory, practice, and process. Software Engineering aims to use the science and technology already available to create products and tools for use.

    Programme Objectives

    The department aims to deliver a strong and coherent Software Engineering program for the development of skilled Software Engineers. Software Engineering graduates should achieve the following educational objectives:

    • Graduates should demonstrate competence in applying Software Engineering knowledge & practices in various phases of software/system development life cycle in their respective professional career.
    • Graduates should demonstrate an ability to work as a member and/or leader in a team with a strong sense of societal context, professional ethics and effective communication skills in professional practice
    • Graduates should demonstrate sustained learning by pursuing life-long learning through graduate studies, professional development or managerial/leadership skills.

    AU Entry Test

    English - 25%
    Analytical and Quantitative - 35%
    Mathematics - 40%

    Duration of the Program

    Minimum: Four Years
    Maximum: Six Years

  • Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence

    Artificial intelligence is a rapidly growing field of computer science. In this field, the machines are designed and programmed in such a manner that they think and act like a human. With the time, artificial intelligence is becoming important for life because of its diverse applications such as banking & financial system, medical science, disease diagnoses & treatment, heavy industry, air transport, gaming zone, surveillance, security, traffic management, and law & order. Because of the diverse applications of artificial intelligence, its demand in industry, academia, and research is increasing.

    Structure of the Program

    The Bachelors of Science in Artificial Intelligence program provides students an opportunity to combine a sound foundation in Artificial Intelligence with concrete and concentrated knowledge in the latest developments in particular areas. The program provides preparation in the advanced principles and processes of computation and training in applying these principles in application areas in industry, science, research, government, and business. The program emphasizes on course work along with practical assignments and projects.

    Programme Objectives

    This program is designed with the specific aims as follows:

    • To provide students the skills in machine learning, artificial intelligence, mathematics, and statistics which are essential for their career in artificial intelligence as professional expert or researcher.
    • To produce artificial intelligence experts to meet the current and future demands of our country for the employment of artificial intelligence to a large number of application areas in health, industry, science, research, government, security, and business.
    • Attract international jobs/companies by creating a local pool of talented and skilled artificial intelligence experts.
    • To contribute towards establishing Pakistan artificial intelligence industry
    • To make Air University Islamabad a pioneer in this field at Pakistan level and one of the renowned University for artificial intelligence internationally.
    • To attract international students from the neighboring countries to bring good name to Air University and improve the University ranking at national and international level
    • To produce graduates that are more employable and highly paid.

Master Programs

  • Master of Science in Data Science (Evening)

    The Master of Science in Data Science MS(DS) program has been designed to give students the option to be part of a data science endeavor that begins with the identification of business processes, determination of data provenance and data ownership, understanding the ecosystem of the business decisions, skill sets and tools that shape the data, making data amenable to analytics, identifying sub-problems, recognizing the technology matrix required for problem resolution, creating incrementally-complex data-driven models and then maintaining them to ultimately leverage them for business growth.

    Programme Objectives

    • To equip students to transform data into actionable insights to make complex business decisions
    • To enable students, understand and analyze a problem and arrive at computable solutions
    • To expose students to the set of technologies that match those solutions
    • To gain hands-on experience on data-centric tools for statistical analysis, visualization and big-data applications at the same rigorous scale as in a practical data science project
    • To understand the implications of handling data in terms of data security and business ethics

    The two-year MS DS program consists of four core courses and four electives from various areas of specializations.

    Deficiency Courses:

    • Programming Fundamentals (Core Programming Course)
    • Data Structures & Algorithms OR Design & Analysis of Algorithms
    • Database Systems
