Chair Department

Greetings and welcome to the Department of Computer Science at Air University, Islamabad!

It is my pleasure and privilege to welcome you to the Department of Computer Science at Air University, Islamabad. Computing is more accessible than ever before and is fundamental to many aspects of human prosperity and wellness. Automation is revolutionizing Health care, Computer Vision, Data Science and Manufacturing Industry.

Computer Science is one of the largest Departments of Air University and offering Undergraduate, Masters and PhD programs. All of the programs are duly verified by the Higher Education Commission and NCEAC of Pakistan. The Department strongly believes in Industry and Academia Collaboration and we have revised our curriculum and added more advanced courses as advised by the Scientific and Industrial Board. Most of our graduating students get good jobs just after completion of their degrees and serving very prestigious national and international organizations. Our FMs have earned degrees from highly reputable national and international universities and fully engaged in research and development projects.

Our FM secured (as PI/Co-PI) around Rs. 73.0m in funding in 2018 only through grants from Planning Commission Govt. of Pakistan. Many of the research proposals are in progress for funding. Our graduates have made meaningful contributions in their respective areas, and secured positions in industry and academia. These are exciting times for computer science researchers, teachers, and students – current and future, who have the opportunity to make a difference in the world we live in as developers, thinkers, and innovators. Computer Science continues to be the most attractive field of study in Pakistan and all over world.

We are proud of our achievements and excited by new opportunities as we aspire to be among the top Computer Science departments in Pakistan. Being a prospective undergraduate/post-graduate student or faculty/staff, we endeavor to provide you the right environment to flourish.

Dr. Mehdi Hassan

Chair Department of Computer Science
