Evening / Weekend GRE Test Preparation Course at AU: Open for Professionals and Students from All Universities including AU Students and Alumni

Dear All,

Asalam o Alikum,

Do you aspire to study in top US universities for Master / MBA / PhD degree on fully funded scholarship (e.g. Fulbright scholarship and Pak-US knowledge corridor scholarship)?

If yes, then Air University International Corporation Office is happy to announce that we are offering the top quality 5-weeks GRE preparation course. For everyone at the lowest prices in the market from 02 March 2020 to 22 March 2020 on first come first serve basis.(the remaining two weeks will be covered after Mid-term exams)

Who should take this course?

The GRE perp course is recommended for 3rd and 4th year students from all UG degree programs, as well as all students and professionals aspiring to go for MS or PhD in US universities.

Tuition Fee:

AU Students / Alumni/ Faculty and Staff Members Rs.6000/-
. Students from other Universities Rs.12,000/-
. Professionals Rs.15,000/-

Instructor:

The course will be conducted by an experienced trainer Mr.Haseeb Sheikh, whose valid GRE score: is 170/170 Quantitative Reasoning and 168/170 verbal Reasoning. The trainer has previously taught 10 GRE perp courses including 2 in Air University.

How much GRE score can win you a MS/PhD Scholarship?

Qualifying score for Pak-US Knowledge corridor PhD scholarship is only 274/340. A GRE score >300/340 is considered decent for acceptance/tuition fee waiver in more than one of the HEC partner US universities and scores >310/340 are considered strong for admission in a number of top 100 US universities.

Why should you take the GRE prep course?

This will enable students to win lavishly funded and most prestigious scholarships in US and eventually get admission in Masters Programs in top ranked US Universities through Fulbright Scholarships, US University funded MS scholarships and/or HEC’s Pak-US Knowledge Corridor Scholarship for MS Leading to PhD.

Which GRE Prep Material / Method Will be Used?

The trainer has developed a specialized course that is more elaborate than any traditional GRE book, tailored specifically for Pakistani students to incorporate fundamental concepts as well as practice material.

Course Details:

Dates:

02 March 2020 to 22 March 2020 (Remaining two weeks after Mid term Exams)

Timings:

1700 hrs to 1900 hrs (Monday to Friday)

Venue:

Room no. G-19, Ground Floor, IAA Building

Who can join:

UG students, PG students, faculty members, professionals, staff members.

Minimum CGPA Required:

CGPA >2.50/4.00

Registration Deadline:

01 March, 2020
